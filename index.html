About us

We upcycle food and plant wastes to protect the environment and planet. Major problem is food loss and wastage causing loss of money , resources, increased hunger rates, greenhouse emissions, overflowing landfills etc. Alternatives are composting at home, selling waste to waste management companies etc.
Public opinion regarding food wastage varies, but there is generally a growing awareness that it is a significant problem that requires solutions. Many people agree that food wastage has negative consequences for the environment, society, and the economy.
We connect customers(restaurants,pg hostels,vegetable markets etc) to the food management systems for upcycling the bio waste.



KEY POINTS

  • Reducing Food Wastage in Restaurants,
  • Food Rescue and Redistribution,
  • Educational Initiatives,
  • Sustainable Practices.


BENEFITS OF FOOD WASTE MANAGEMENT

  • Reduce emissions,
  • Conserve resources,
  • Promote green technology,
  • Limit waste,
  • Protect biodiversity,
  • Sustainable practices and eco-friendly choices
  • Promote recycling,
  • Eco-friendly practices for health.


FEW METHODS

  • Anaerobic composting
  • vermicomposting
  • aerobic composting
  • Electricity from bio gas
  • Vehicle fuels and etc



WHAT WE DO

We are revolutionizing waste management by transforming food waste into sustainable, recyclable products.

  • Our innovative solutions make responsible waste disposal not just a goal but a reality, turning waste into a valuable resource.
  • Customers not only get rid of their wastes but in return get recyclable products made out of that same waste.
  • We connect customers who have bio waste with the local food waste management companies through this website.
